About Me

Welcome to my profile! I am based out of Chicago, IL and pursuing a software development certification from Rutgers University. I most recently worked as a project manager at Diageo where I lead a crossfunctional team into commercialize the 2019 Whiskey of the year (George Dickel Bottled in Bond). Prior to Diageo, I worked at Nest Fragrances as the primary product development engineer where I helped design and develop packaging for all components (candles, perfume, lotion, soap, etc).
